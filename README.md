# Duplicate-Catcher

### 🚀 [**Open the Live App →**](https://sumankayal247.github.io/Duplicate-Catcher/)

---

Catches duplicate **videos** or **images** — even the same content saved under a
different name, resized, or re-encoded.

1. Pick **Video** or **Images** and bulk-add your files.
2. It finds the duplicates and shows each one **beside its original** to review.
3. Refute any pair you disagree with.
4. Click **"Yeah, That's all of them"** to download a **zip of just the originals**.

Everything runs in your browser — your files never leave your device.
